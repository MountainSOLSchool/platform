# Class forms & contacts

The **Class Forms and Contacts** screen (`/admin/report`) gives you, per class, a
quick look at who's enrolled plus the printable forms and contact emails you need to
run the term. Pick the term from the **Semester** dropdown at the top.

<Shot slug="class-forms-contacts" caption="The Class Forms and Contacts screen." />

Each class is a row showing its **Name**, **# Enrolled Students**, and **Start** /
**End** dates. On the right are two buttons:

| Button | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **View Forms** | Opens the class's forms (the roster / sign-in and related printouts) to view, print, or save. |
| **View Emails** | Opens the list of enrolled students' contact emails for that class — handy for sending a class-wide message. |

Sort by any column header to find a class quickly.

## See who's enrolled at a glance

Click a class row (or the arrow on its left) to **expand** it and see the enrolled
students right there — no need to open the printable forms. Click again to collapse it.

<Shot slug="class-roster-expanded" caption="Expanding a class shows its enrolled students and their key details." />

For each student you'll see:

- **Name and age**
- **Parent/guardian contact** — name, phone, and email
- **Add-ons** they signed up for, such as **early dropoff** or **late pickup**
- **Safety details** — allergies (highlighted when life-threatening), medications,
  the family's **code word**, and who's **authorized for pickup**

This is the fastest way to answer "who's in this class and what do I need to know
about them?" For a printable copy to bring to class, use **View Forms**.

> Use **View Emails** to grab the whole class's email list in one go, rather than
> copying addresses one student at a time.
