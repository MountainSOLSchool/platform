Summer Solstice Workday and Celebration

Location: Chestnut Ridge Park and Campground

Description:
Our Summer Solstice event will have two parts: a work day to support Chestnut Ridge Park and our annual summer solstice potluck party. You, your family, and any guests you would like to bring are welcome to come to either or both parts of our summer solstice event!

From 12-4pm we will have a project work day, led by Mountain SOL staff and students but open to the public, in support of Chestnut Ridge Park. We will be doing projects including gardening and maintenance.

From 5-9pm more people will gather, and we will have our big summer solstice party! There will be potluck food, music, fire, and activities. This is a favorite event for building our Mountain SOL community, as many new students and older Mountain SOL alums all gather together to celebrate outdoors!
