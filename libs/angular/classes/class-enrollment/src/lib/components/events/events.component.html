Summer Solstice Celebration and Skills Test

Location: Chestnut Ridge Park and Campground

Description:
Join us for our annual Mountain SOL community summer celebration! This special event includes our skills test out, where students can demonstrate their mastery of various outdoor skills. The skills testing will be led by Jason and Lila, along with other experienced instructors. Students will have the opportunity to test out of required units for their paths.

All Mountain SOL community members are welcome to participate in this summer evening of achievement and celebration.
