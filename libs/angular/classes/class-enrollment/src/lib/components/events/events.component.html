Summer Solstice Celebration and Skills Test

Location: Chestnut Ridge Park and Campground

Description:
Join us for a Mountain SOL community summer celebration! All students, past alumni, staff, volunteers, parents, and new friends are welcome! We will have potluck food, a music jam, activities, and a campfire graduation ceremony for any students who are graduating from a path. This year, we will also have a skills test, open to all students, which will let students test out of units required for their paths. Students will have the opportunity to complete various challenges, which will count as credit for different units, including fire, knots, tarp shelters, and more!
